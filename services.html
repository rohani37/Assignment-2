Annual Dental Check-Up Package*

This package includes the following services:

  • Physical Examination: A full assessment of your dental health, checking for illness, abnormalities, or discomfort.
  • Cleanings: Professional removal of plaque and tartar.
  • X-Rays: Diagnostic imaging to detect hidden dental issues.
  • Dental Check-Up: Evaluation of dental health and recommendations for care.
  • Nutritional Guidance: Advice on diet and dental health.
  • Blood Tests / Lab Work (if needed): Additional diagnostic tests based on age or history.
